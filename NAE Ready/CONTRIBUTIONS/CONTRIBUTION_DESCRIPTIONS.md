Three versions of contribution descriptions for different contexts (concise, detailed, portfolio).

## Option 3 — Portfolio / Case Study Style

**Co-Author of the Neural Agency Engine (NAE)**: Led the end-to-end design and conceptual development of NAE, including agent structure, AI trading strategies, and system workflow. ## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Each Version

**Option 1 (Concise)**:
- LinkedIn headline
- Resume summary section
- Brief introductions
- Twitter/LinkedIn bio
- Email signature

**Option 2 (Detailed)**:
- Resume experience section
- Project descriptions
- Technical portfolio
- Grant applications
- Job applications

**Option 3 (Portfolio)**:
- Portfolio website
- Case study presentations
- Detailed project documentation
- Investor pitches
- Conference abstracts

### Customization Tips

- **For Technical Roles**: Emphasize architecture, algorithms, system design
- **For Business Roles**: Emphasize strategy, objectives, outcomes
- **For Leadership Roles**: Emphasize direction, collaboration, vision
- **For Research Roles**: Emphasize innovation, conceptualization, design
